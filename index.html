Our Story

Every celebration deserves something made with care.

The Baking Studio was founded by Priyanka Verma, a passionate home baker whose love for cake artistry grew into a boutique home bakery.

Every cake is handcrafted, completely eggless, and freshly prepared only after your order is confirmed.

From birthdays and anniversaries to surprise celebrations, every creation reflects quality, creativity and attention to detail.

Within the first year, The Baking Studio proudly handcrafted and delivered over 40 custom cakes across Kasauli and nearby areas, creating memorable moments one celebration at a time.
